Old Fashioned Recipe

A classic old fashioned. What else is there to say? Adds a bit of class to any meal or activity. For this you'll need:

  • Simple syrup
  • Water
  • Bitters
  • Ice
  • Bourbon
  • An orange slice
  • Optional: Cherry
  1. Pour 2 teaspoons of simple syrup, 2 dashes of bitters, 1 teaspoon of water, and 2oz of bourbon into glass.
  2. Stir
  3. Add ice
  4. Add orange slice
  5. Optional: Add a cherry
